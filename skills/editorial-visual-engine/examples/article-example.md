# Example: Article to Editorial Field Guide

## User request

> Turn this article into an educational LinkedIn graphic and caption.

## Expected route

1. Read the article and its internal sections.
2. Extract the thesis, evidence, steps, dates, and author claims.
3. Recommend a field guide, step playbook, comparison, or commentary board.
4. Use EV-01 or EV-05 when the article contains several practical units.
5. Compress the article into exact on-image copy without copying full paragraphs.
6. Attribute claims that belong to the author.
7. Write a LinkedIn caption that adds context beyond the graphic.

## Failure conditions

- Treating the article as an independently verified source
- Filling gaps with general web knowledge
- Copying long passages into the visual
- Using a decorative illustration that does not summarise the article
